Frog Goes To Space

In this game, you play as Fröggie, a young frog with a simple goal of going to space. Join Fröggie as they jump and swing through different levels, all while collecting star pieces, on their path to space.

Click the game window to start.

Made with Godot Engine by Allison Christensen, Jacob Denyes, Cooper Harasyn, and Fiona Heysel.
