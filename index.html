Frog Goes To Space

In this game, you play as Fröggie, a young frog with a simple goal of going to space. In the completed game, the player will control Fröggie as they jump and swing through five different levels, all while collecting star pieces, on their path to space.

In this short demo, there is only one playable level. Several art assets and animations are incorrect or missing; we are looking mostly for feedback on the core movement mechanics (walking, jumping, swinging), and we hope that the lack of animations does not detract from the core gameplay experience. An explanation of the controls can be found below the game window. You will need to click on the game window in order to play. We hope you enjoy!

Once you have completed the demo, please fill out this survey. Your responses will not be published in any manner, but a summary of the results will be used in reports to the Queen's ELEC 498 teaching staff.

Instructions:

Use the arrow keys to move left and right.

Use the Z key to jump.

Use the X key to shoot your tongue and swing from platforms.

When swinging, press X to stop swinging, or press Z to jump out!
